## Overview

Teamgloomy have teamed up with Mellow/Flymaker to produce ESP32 adapters
They can be purchased through [aliexpress](https://mellow.aliexpress.com).  
There are two adapters that support the following boards:

### Adapter 1

- GTR
- SKR Pro
- Fly-407ZG
